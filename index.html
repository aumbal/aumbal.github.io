Howdy! I'm Alyssa!

UX/UI Designer • Illustrator

Pleasure to meet you!

I’m Alyssa, an illustrator and UX/UI Designer that loves to use visual communication, illustration and design to enrich relationships between people and technology. As a child, making things look pretty and drawing were huge passions of mine, so why not use these passions to bring joy in people’s lives! From making interfaces beautiful to creating fun illustrations, I hope to create meaningful experiences that bring a smile to people’s faces.

I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University with a concentration in Design in Vancouver, BC. I currently work at The Peak SFU as an associate illustrator. You can find me at local hot pot restaurants, posting my illustrations on my instagram, or watching ducks swim at local ponds!
