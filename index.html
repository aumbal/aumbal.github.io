Who am I?

As a child, I was always drawn to aesthetics. Making things look pretty and illustration were huge passions of mine and I always dreamed of becoming a visual artist since I believed that beautiful things can engage people. I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University with a concentration in Design in Vancouver, BC to further develop my craft and better learn how to shape aesthetics so people can enjoy the product they are using.

I am proficient in illustration tools such as Adobe Illustrator, and prototyping tools such as Figma, as well as in front-end development with HTML/CSS.

You can find me at local hot pot restaurants, posting illustrations on my Instagram, or watching ducks swim at local ponds!
