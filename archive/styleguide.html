Alyssa's Design Portfolio

I’m Alyssa, an illustrator and interdisciplinary designer hoping to create meaningful and human-centered experiences in people’s lives and integrate their life stories into beautifully crafted interfaces. Through my projects, I hope to promote wellness and show different parts of our world. So, do you want to go on an adventure with me?

I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, BC. You can find me at local hot pot restaurants, learning Japanese, or doodling in my beloved sketchbooks!

My Ethos

I want to show my skills for both the UX/UI and/or Illustration jobs. Since I will be aiming for a career in these fields, I want my portfolio to have a fun appearance, using a bright color scheme comprising of my favourite colors for an inviting feel, and a layout that is easy to navigate. Easy navigation for my portfolio will make it easier for my work and projects to be presented right away. The projects I will showcase are relevant to these fields as well. I have experience with UX/UI through Hackathons which I will showcase, as well as plenty of illustrations ranging from vector art, isometric art, ink, and digital art with a pen and tablet!
