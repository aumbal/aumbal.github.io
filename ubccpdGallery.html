Introduction

UBC Continuing Professional Development (CPD) is an academic unit under University of British Columbia’s Faculty of Medicine. UBC CPD offers online course modules for practicing physicians to allow them to continue their medical education, imbue professional growth, and quality of practice. UBC CPD’s mission is to improve health care practices across Canada and internationally. UBC CPD is accredited by the Committee on Accreditation of Canadian Medical Education to accredit programs and provide credits to practicing physicians.

I had the opportunity to be an Interactive Design Intern for UBC CPD under the Creative Learning department. Throughout my internship, I created a plethora of illustrations, such as updating course banners to fit UBC CPD’s current branding, graphic learning material and spot illustrations for our online courses. I used Adobe Illustrator to create the illustrations and used our Learning Management System, Moodle, to upload the illustrations to our eLearning Site.
