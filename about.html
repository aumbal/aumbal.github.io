My background is in user experience design. I explore solutions to tackle user needs and frustrations, use communication design/illustration for visual storytelling and strive for accessibility and inclusivity. I love to experiment with different styles and learn new software to discover different lenses of design.

I’m currently pursuing my undergraduate degree at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University with a concentration in Design. I currently work at The Peak SFU as a freelance illustrator. I previously worked at UBC CPD as an interactive designer where I worked on designing and developing online learning modules for physicians in Canada.

I am proficient in illustration tools such as Adobe Illustrator, and prototyping tools such as Figma, as well as in front-end development with HTML/CSS.

When I’m not designing, you can find me at local hot pot restaurants, playing Animal Crossing, posting isometric illustrations on my instagram, and watching ducks swim!
